Hello Hello! Welcome to the little corner of the internet called Cece's Mini Gaming Blog. This mini-blog is where I, Cece, will write reveiews on games that I come across, talk about video game media, and sometimes share music playlists as well. This blog started on 5 December 2022 and met it's end at 9 December due to the news that the software atom.io was sunsetting/closing down.

Mission / Purpose

The original intent of this little blog is for the assignment in Professor Kapper's "Fundamentals of Interactive Design" class called "Create your own site using CSS.". Using this website, I was going to show off what I had learned in both HTML and CSS. However, when the deadline came for the website to be uploaded, this website did not meet the assignment requirements.

What is Cece working on?

A review of a mobile game targeted for girls known as Time Princess.

Rules and Notices

Ah yes. The rules section. Here are the rules that I abide by. Please spare a moment to read them.

  1. I am not qualified to give legitimate and true opinions unlike other websites.
  2. Please do not rush me for reviews / opinion pieces. A degree-seeking life is a busy one!
  3. I am not too keen on horror video games or media outside of Halloween. Do not ask for reviews on them outside of that window.
  4. Please be advised that I do have biases of own and that they may cloud judgement.

Enjoy your stay!
