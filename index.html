Hello Hello! Welcome to the little corner of the internet called Cece's Mini Gaming Blog. This mini-blog is where I, Cece, will write reveiews on games that I come across, talk about video game media, and sometimes share music playlists as well. This blog first started on 5 December 2022.

Mission / Purpose

The original intent of this little blog is for the assignment in Professor Kapper's "Fundamentals of Interactive Design" class called "Create your own site using CSS.".

Rules

Ah yes. The rules section. Here are the rules that I abide by. Please spare a moment to read them.

  1. I am not qualified to give legitimate and true opinions unlike other websites.

  3. I am not too keen on horror video games or media outside of Halloween. Do not ask for reviews on them outside of that window.
  4. Please be advised that I do have biases of own and that they may cloude judgement.

Enjoy your stay!
